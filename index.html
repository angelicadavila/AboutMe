About Me

I am a PhD student at the Computer Architecture Group of the Informatics and System Engineering Department at the University of Zaragoza, Spain. My research interest focus on heterogeneous systems, specially on FPGA devices as accelerators for load balancing algorithms.
Since october 2017, I am studying my PhD with a scholarship from Santander bank and University of Zaragoza. In november 2019, I spend 3 months in the Technische Universität Dresden with a HiPeac collaboration grant.
From 2014 and 2017 I was working on digital and analog design of electronic circuits in SENA (National Service of Education), Colombia. In 2014 I got my Master degree as Electronic Engineer in the University of Valle, Colombia.
